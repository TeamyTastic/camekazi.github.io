Why three loops matter

Single-loop keeps you moving (fix immediate friction).
Double-loop makes you smarter (build better rules).
Triple-loop makes the system smarter (evolve how you learn).

Most systems only have single-loop. Adding double-loop creates compounding. Adding triple-loop creates meta-compounding ‚Äî the learning system learns how to learn.
