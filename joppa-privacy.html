Joppa — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 February 2026

What the skill does

Joppa is a private Alexa skill that controls WiiM speakers and a Samsung Frame TV on a local home network. It is not published to the public Alexa Skills Store.

Data we collect

None. Joppa does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Voice requests are processed by Amazon Alexa and forwarded to a local server on your home network. No data leaves your network beyond the standard Alexa service.

Third-party services

Joppa communicates only with devices on the local network (WiiM speakers, Samsung TV, Philips Hue) and the Wikimedia Commons API (for public-domain artwork). No user data is shared with any third party.

Account linking

This skill does not use account linking and does not require login credentials.

Children

This skill is not directed at children under 13.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the skill developer via the Alexa Skills page.
